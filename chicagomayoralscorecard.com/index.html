Chicago Mayoral Election Scorecard

With so many candidates in play for the seat vacated by Mayor Daley, a cheat sheet is awfully handy.
The data for this list is compiled and updated by Progress Illinois, though the Scorecard is unaffiliated.
Clicking on a candidate's name will search for the latest news about them.
