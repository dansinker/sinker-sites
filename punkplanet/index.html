Punk Planet was awesome

it changed a lot of things. then it ended.

Your best bet in tracking down issues of Punk Planet is through Quimbys (they have more issues than are listed on their site, so if you're looking for something specific, give them a call).

A full run of all 80 issues of Punk Planet is held in the collections of the Library of Congress, the Newberry Library, and the Experience Music Project.

We miss you all.
