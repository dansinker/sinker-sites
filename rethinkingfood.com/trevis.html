Small Gardens for Small Spaces

By Trevis Loosbrock

Documentary film maker Trevis Loosbrock sources and builds a window farm at Columbia College Chicago.

Many people in the city want to be more connected to their food by either going to a farmers market, or growing their own produce this can be accomplished. The problem is that in the city most people don't have the space to have a full garden, or don't have the money to buy everything from a farmers market. A windowfarm is an easy solution to both. This short documentary will show the benefits of a windowfarm and the easy steps it take to build one
