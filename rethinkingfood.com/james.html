Feed

By Mark Munger

Discover where some of the food commonly found on your table comes from and how it gets there in this interactive data visualization.

ore and more people are thinking about where their food comes from. This concern has spurned on the Locavore and Slow Food movements as people work to develop a personal connection with their food and the people who produce it. In this environment, knowledge is power. This interactive data visualization maps out several different models for food distribution: a local co-op, an organic CSA program that imports foods from small farms across the country, and a local branch of a Supermarket chain
