Chicago’s Local and Sustainable Resources

By Maria Tzeka

Finding a place where local produce is being sold is not easy, nor common. Check out if there’s anything around your area.

All these establishments on this map make purchasing local and sustainable produce in the Chicagoland area possible. As consumers, where we put our money has a big part in foreshadowing the future. If we want to see a change in the ways food is being grown and distributed, it is important for us to support such places as on the map.
