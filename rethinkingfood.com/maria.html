Chicago's Local and Sustainable Resources

By Maria Tzeka

Maps for the eco and health conscious locavore.

Chicago's Locavore Hot Spots points out all the places where purchasing local and sustainable produce in the Chicagoland area is made possible. As consumers, where we put our money has a big part in foreshadowing the future. If we want to see a change in the ways food is being grown and distributed, it is important for us to support these places on the map.
