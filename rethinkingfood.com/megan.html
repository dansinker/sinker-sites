The Real Cost of Cheap Food

By Megan McEntee

How can you make a chicken for just $1.88 a pound? You can't. We uncover the real cost of your chicken dinner.

Every year millions of Americans go to their local grocery store chain and buy food for incredibly cheap. It doesn’t seem possible to cover all the expenses that preceded the food’s journey to the store for the low price consumers are paying. Following a chicken through the industrialized food system, this documentary focuses on explaining the dangerous hidden costs of food production in the United States.
