Cooking Local

By Brent Day

Where can you find locally sourced ingredients in Chicago? Take a bike ride to the Green Grocer and talk with Owner Cassie Green about her suppliers of local food and drinks.

Bringing local food home is as easy as a bike ride to a one stop local food shop like the Green Grocer in West Town. Join Brent Day as he takes advantage of a beautiful autumn day and visits with Cassie Green, owner of Green Grocer Chicago. Cassie tells stories of her local suppliers of meat and produce as she build meals for a vegetarian on a budget as well as a meat lover’s dream. After a quick bike ride home it’s time to hit the kitchen and enjoy a delicious home cooked, locally sourced meal.
